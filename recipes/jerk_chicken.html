Jerk chicken

Description

A Jamaican classic made with chicken marinated in a spicy, flavorul marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
  • 3 medium scallions, chopped
  • 2 Scotch bonnet chiles, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp five-spice powder
  • 1 tbsp allspice berries coarsley ground
  • 1 tbsp coarsley ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 10 chicekn leg and thigh quarters

Method

  1. In a food processor combine onion, scallions, garlic, five-spice powder, allspice, pepper, thyme, nutmeg, and salt; process to a coarse paste.
  2. With food processor running add soy sauce and oil in a steady stream, pour marinade into a large shallow dish, add chicken and turn to coat<. Cover and refrigerate over night./li>
  3. Light a grill and grill the chicken over a medium hot fire, turning ocassionally, until well browned and cooked through 35-40 minutes.
