Collard greens

Description

A wholesome southern classic, made up of collard greens cooked in a savory pot likkur

Ingredients

  • Collard greens
  • 1 Onion diced
  • 1 tbsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1 fully cooked smoked turkey leg
  • Chicken broth
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Smoked Applewood salt
  • Distilled white vinegar

Method

  1. Wash greens and remove stems
  2. Cut greens into 1/2 inch squares
  3. In a large pot, heat olive oil. Once hot, add in onions and cook until tender and translucent. Add in garlic and red pepper flakes.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and add smoked turkey leg, bring to a boil and add greens to the pot.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for one hour or until greens are soft, stirring several times while cooking.
  6. Add black pepper, smoked salt and vinegar and stir before serving.
