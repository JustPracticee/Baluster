Steamed Dumplings

Description

A south Asian staple consisting of steamed dumpling wrappers filled with delicious savory ground pork mixture.

Ingredients

  • 12 pieces homemade or store bought dumplings
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp water or broth for dumplings
  • 1 tbsp doubanjiang
  • 1/2 tbsp chili oil or chili garlic sauce
  • 2-3 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp Chinese black vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Seasame oil
  • 1/2 tsp Sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • Chopped fresh scallions

Method

  1. Mix sauce ingredients together adjusting for desired salt and spice level
  2. Steam the dumplings over a pot in a bamboo steamer lined with parchement paper.
  3. Drain dumplings and let dry on a paper towel, and serve with dipping sauce garnished with scallions.
